This is where the equations of motion for the fields are actually evaluated.

The first and second time derivatives of the fields are computed in accordance with the Klein-Gordon equation, which is written in program units and transformed to momentum-space. Note that the choice of program units has eliminated the first-time-derivative term from the second-time-derivative equation.
